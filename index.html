Local experts, personal service

Ilya Tours & Safaris Ltd is a locally owned tour operator based in Tanga, on Tanzania's north-east coast — close to the Kenyan border and just a short hop from Zanzibar, Pemba and Dar es Salaam.

We arrange everything from camping and lodge safaris in the Serengeti, Ngorongoro Crater and Lake Manyara, to Kilimanjaro and Mt Meru climbs, Usambara mountain treks, beach holidays and community-based cultural tours. Many of our trips support local guides, food producers and eco-conscious partners along the way.

Whether you're backpacking or looking for comfort and luxury, we design each trip around you — and if you're travelling anywhere else in Tanzania, we can arrange that too.
